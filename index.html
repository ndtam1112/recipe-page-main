Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, herbs, vegetables, or cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs : In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat the pan : In a non-stick skillet, heat the butter or oil over medium heat until hot.
  3. Cook the eggs : Pour the beaten eggs into the pan. Let them cook undisturbed for a few moments until they start to set around the edges.
  4. Add fillings : If using, sprinkle your desired fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold the omelette : Carefully fold the other half over the fillings and cook for another minute or so until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully cooked.
  6. Serve : Slide the omelette onto a plate, cut in half if desired, and serve immediately.
